The Science Behind the Scan

This neurofeedback system measures electrical activity in your brain using EEG (electroencephalography) technology. This non-invasive approach has been used in research and clinical settings for decades, providing reliable insights into cognitive function.

Sensors Detect Brainwaves

Electrodes placed on your scalp measure electrical signals from different brain regions.

Data Is Analyzed

Software processes frequencies including beta (focus), alpha (calm alertness), theta (creativity), and delta (deep rest).

Patterns Are Identified

Your unique brainwave signature reveals how your nervous system responds to stress and demand.

You Get Actionable Insight

Specific recommendations based on your patterns, not generic meditation advice.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this medical?

No. This is a performance-oriented neurofeedback experience designed for personal and professional development, not a medical diagnosis or treatment. Think of it as performance analytics for your brain, similar to how athletes use VO2 max testing or lactate threshold analysis.

How private is my data?

Completely confidential. Nothing is shared without your explicit permission. Your scan results belong to you exclusively. No third-party access, no data sales, no marketing lists. This is between you and your results.

Does it hurt?

It's entirely non-invasive, silent, and actually quite relaxing. You sit comfortably while wearing a headset with sensors. No needles, no shocks, no discomfort. Most founders report feeling unusually calm during the process.

Do I need preparation?

Just come rested if possible. Coffee is optional — we scan you in your natural state, whatever that is. Avoid alcohol the night before. Otherwise, show up as you normally are. Authentic data beats optimized data.
